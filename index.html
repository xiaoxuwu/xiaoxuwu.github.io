Who I am

Data is beautiful. Data is powerful. Data is my passion.

Every data point - from the expected to the strange outliers - is part of an overarching story. I like to think of them as pieces of a jigsaw puzzle, just waiting to be put together.

Uncovering this mystery is something I obsess over.

I find it incredible that data can tell not only a story but a beautiful story that can make the world a better place. And to think of all the stories still untold! I wouldn't miss it for the world.

Work hard, play hard.

Over this last summer I worked a full-time data science internship and a part-time software engineering position for two demanding startups. This was a very rewarding experience, and I feel that I've grown and learned a lot as a result.

While I loved the work I was doing, I had less and less time to myself and with friends.

What I found was when I left work earlier to go out for a night or join Game of Thrones/Rick and Morty watch parties with my friends, I went back to work more rejuvinated and focused. It helped me get more done in less time. Most importantly, I was happier.

Finding the right work-life balance has been difficult for me, but I'm happy to say I've made progress. Work can be addicting, but I've learned that having fun in other areas is just as important.
